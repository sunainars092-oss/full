Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport played indoors or outdoors, involving two (singles) or four (doubles) players hitting a shuttlecock over a high net. It requires agility, speed, and precision, with players using light rackets to score points by landing the shuttle in the opponent’s court. Popular worldwide, it is an Olympic sport known for improving cardiovascular health, reflexes, and overall fitness.
